## 🔬 기능 콤플렉스

### 💎 BRIGHTENING COMPLEX
- Niacinamide 2.5% + Alpha-Arbutin 0.8% + Pearl Extract 0.5%
- 멜라닌 생성 억제 + 자연스러운 광택

- PDRN 0.15% + Saccharomyces Ferment 1.5% + Bifida 0.5%

- Centella 0.8% + Panthenol 0.8% + Allantoin 0.15%

- Menthoxypropanediol 0.3% + Cucumber Extract 0.5%

- 저자극 쿨링감, 상쾌한 세안 경험
