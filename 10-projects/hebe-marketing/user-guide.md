## For Brand Managers / Danh cho Brand Managers

### Your Responsibilities / Nhiem vu cua ban

1. **Submit Monthly Plans** - Create and submit your marketing plan each month
2. **Track Spending** - Record all expenses in the Spending Tracker
3. **Attach Evidence** - Always include receipts/invoices (Google Drive links)

---

## How to Submit a Monthly Plan

### Step 1: Go to Monthly Plans Database
- Open "HeBe Marketing Hub" in Notion
- Click "Monthly Plans"

### Step 2: Create New Plan
- Click "+ New" button
- Fill in the template:

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|---------------|
| Plan Title | `[Brand] [Country] - [Month] [Year]` (e.g., "Medianswer VN - Jan 2026") |
| Brand | Select your brand |
| Country | Vietnam or Cambodia |
| Month | Select the month |
| Submitted By | Tag yourself |

### Step 3: Enter Budget Items
Fill in the amounts (in KRW) for each category:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Seeding | Free products to KOCs |
| KOL/Influencer | Influencer bookings |
| Paid Ads | Meta, TikTok, Google Ads |
| Live Commerce | Vo Ha Linh, Shopee Live |
| Affiliate | TikTok Affiliate |
| Content Production | Video/photo production |
| Offline/Popup | Popup stores, OOH |
| Trade Promo | Retailer promotions |
| Other | Any other expenses |

### Step 4: Add Notes
In the "Notes" field, briefly explain:
- Why you're allocating budget this way
- Key campaigns planned
- Expected outcomes

### Step 5: Submit for Approval
- Change "Approval Status" to **"Submitted"**
- Tag @Mike in comments

### Step 6: Wait for Approval
- Mike will review and change status to "Approved" or "Rejected"
- If rejected, check comments for feedback and revise

---

## How to Record Spending

### After Your Plan is Approved:

1. Go to **"Spending Tracker"** database
2. Click **"+ New"**
3. Fill in:

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|---------------|
| Item | Description of expense (e.g., "KOL @nguyen_beauty - Jan content") |
| Monthly Plan | Link to your approved monthly plan |
| Brand | Select brand |
| Category | Select matching category |
| Amount (KRW) | Amount in Korean Won |
| Amount (Local) | Amount in VND or USD |
| Currency | VND or USD |
| Date | Date of expense |
| Receipt/Evidence | Google Drive link to invoice/receipt |
| Submitted By | Tag yourself |

4. Your **Remaining Budget** will automatically update

---

## Important Rules

### DO:
- Submit plans by the **25th of previous month** (e.g., Feb plan by Jan 25)
- Always attach evidence for every expense
- Use correct categories
- Ask Mike if unsure

### DON'T:
- Spend before approval
- Exceed your approved budget
- Change approved plans without re-approval
